Tokneneng

Ingredients

  • 6 hard-boiled quail eggs or chicken eggs
  • 1 cup all purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 teaspoon annatto powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • Cooking oil

Instructions

  1. Peel the boiled quail eggs and dry with a paper towel.
  2. In a bowl, mix the flour, cornstarch, annatto powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. Slowly add water to the dry ingredients while stirring to create a smooth, thick batter.
  4. Dip the eggs into the batter.
  5. Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat.
  6. Deep fry the eggs until gold and crispy. Drain excess oil using paper towel.
  7. Serve with vinegar.

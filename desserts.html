Ingredients/Procedures

Halo-Halo

Ingredients

  • 1 cup shaved ice
  • 1/4 cup sweetened red beans
  • 1/4 cup sweetened jackfruit
  • 1/4 cup sweetened banana
  • 1/4 cup nata de coco
  • 1/4 cup leche flan
  • 1 scoop ube ice cream
  • 1 tablespoon macapuno
  • 1/4 cup sweetened coconut strips
  • Evaporated milk

Instructions

  1. add the sweetened red beans, jackfruit, banana, nata de coco, leche flan, and macapuno.
  2. Add shaved ice on top.
  3. Put ube ice cream and a drizzle of evaporated milk.
  4. Lastly add sprinkles and syrups on top.
  5. Serve well.

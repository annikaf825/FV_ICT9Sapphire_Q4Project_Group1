Pinakbet

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound pork belly or liempo, sliced into small pieces
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • 1 cup squash, cubed
  • 1 cup eggplant, sliced
  • 1 cup ampalaya, sliced
  • 1/2 cup okra, whole or sliced
  • 1/2 cup sitW, cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 2 tablespoons bagoong
  • 1/2 cup water
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Heat the cooking oil in a pan and sautee the garlic, onion, and tomatoes until it softens.
  2. Add pork belly and cook until it looks light brown.
  3. Mix in bagoong and stir.
  4. Add the water and simmer
  5. Add the vegetablesslowly, start with the squash, then eggplant, ampalaya okra, and sitaw Cook for about 10-15 minutes or until the vegetables are tender.
  6. Season with salt and pepper if you want.
  7. Serve hot and best enjoyed with rice.
